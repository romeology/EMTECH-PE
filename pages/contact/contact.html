How to Use the Application:

1. Click on the Calculator tab.
2. Choose your exercise activity of choice by clicking on it and choosing an activity from the dropdown menu.
3. Enter your weight in kilograms.
4. Enter the duration of your exercise in minutes.
5. Click the button to see your amount of burned calories and amount of weight lost.

For additional information regarding calories, exercise, and how it relates to METs, refer to the Home tab and its attached link.
