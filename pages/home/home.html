Frequently Asked Questions:
1. What is a MET?

MET or Metabolic Equivalent of Task is the ratio of your working metabolic rate relative to your resting metabolic rate. Which Metabolic Rate is the rate of energy expended per unit of time. One MET is your energy spent when you're sitting or resting.

2. What is the connection between METs and calories?

Calories is what you burn and consume each day. But METs can appoximately calculate the estimated calories burned by doing a certain activity.
