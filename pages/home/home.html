This app would be will be offering service that will be calculating the calories burned from doing different activities. To calculate the burned calories from doing various activities, the calculator will be utilizing Metabolic Equivalent of Task or METs. However, the calculator would only be giving a rough estimate on how much a person would be burning calories. MET is the ratio of your working metabolic rate to your resting metabolic rate.
